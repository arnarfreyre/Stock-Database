About Implied Volatility Surface

The implied volatility surface shows how option-implied volatility varies across different strike prices and expiration dates. It provides insights into market expectations and can reveal volatility smile patterns and term structure.

  • X-axis (Time to Maturity): Days or years until option expiration
  • Y-axis (Strike Price): Option strike prices
  • Z-axis (Implied Volatility): Annualized implied volatility
  • Color Gradient: Visualizes volatility levels (cooler colors = lower IV, warmer = higher IV)

Note: The surface is calculated using the Black-Scholes model with Newton-Raphson optimization. Only liquid options with sufficient trading volume are included in the calculation.
